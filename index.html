About Me

I'm a native North Carolinian with a penchant for travel and an honest interest in community. That wanderlust led me through the halls of academic medical research, into the trials of grassroots social organizing on Chicago's west side, and towards a rewarding technology and market research career.

Throughout these experiences, I gained an appreciation for and fascination with the undeniable necessity of web technologies in todays's world. From violence prevention, to health disparities research, I worked with professionals to introduce web based resources into work that traditionally didn't rely on them. As I continue to identify my own career interests, one thing is very clear - it will be in tech!

Please find out more about my work below.
